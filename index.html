About Nightfall

🌙 Who We Are

Nightfall is an open space — built for real people who want real connection. Whether you're looking to make new friends, share a laugh, dive into deep conversation, or simply find someone to listen when things feel heavy — you belong here. We don't judge. We don't gate-keep. The night holds space for everyone, and so do we. This is a place where you can simply be yourself, find your people, and never feel alone under the stars.

✦ guidelines ✦

The Nightfall Code

We keep it relaxed. Just a few things to keep the night peaceful for everyone.

01
✌️
Respect Each Other
Treat everyone the way you'd want to be treated. No hate speech, no targeted harassment, no putting people down. Disagreements are fine — cruelty isn't.
Core Rule
02
🚫
No Discrimination
We don't care about your race, gender, sexuality, religion, or background. Every person is welcome here — keep it that way. Bigotry of any kind gets you gone.
Core Rule
03
📌
Right Channel Vibes
Keep chats relevant to the channel they're in. It keeps things easy to follow for everyone — don't worry, there's a channel for basically everything.
Relaxed
04
🔇
No Spamming
Don't flood the chat, don't repeat yourself endlessly, and please — no ear-splitting audio in voice chats. Keep it easy on everyone's eyes and ears.
Relaxed
05
🔞
NSFW Stays in Its Lane
Adult content belongs in designated channels only — and only if you're of age. Keep it out of general spaces so everyone stays comfortable.
Core Rule
06
🧠
Be Mindful When Venting
We fully support you venting and seeking support — that's what we're here for. Just be mindful of triggering content and use content warnings where needed. Everyone's healing at a different pace.
With Care
07
🛡️
Listen to the Mods
Our mods are here to keep the peace, not power-trip. If a mod says something, it's for a reason. If you think something's unfair, you can appeal — calmly.
Core Rule
08
🎉
Have Fun
Seriously — this is meant to be a good time. Laugh, be weird, be yourself. The rules exist to protect the good vibes, not kill them. Welcome to Nightfall. ✨
